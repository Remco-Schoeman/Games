How to play

  • The cream shape on the green baseplate is the figure you must build.
  • Tap a brick in the palette to pick it up, then tap the plate to drop it. The brick anchors at its top-left.
  • Bricks have to stay inside the cream shape — no overhang and no overlap.
  • Tap a brick that's already on the plate to send it back to the palette.
  • Use every brick in the palette to win.
