Celtic Knot

Puzzle1/1
Edges0
Tap segments between the dots. Numbers say how many sides of that cell are in the loop.

The knot is whole.

How to play

  • Tap the short segments between dots to toggle them on or off.
  • The number in a cell is how many of that cell's four sides must be part of the loop.
  • The selected segments must form a single closed loop — every dot is used by zero or two segments, and the whole thing is connected.
  • Tap Check when you think it's done.
