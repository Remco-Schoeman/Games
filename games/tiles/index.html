      How to play

      • The board has a 6 × 8 grid of stacked tiles.
      • Tap a tile to select it, then tap another tile whose visible layer matches in both pattern and colour.
      • Matched pairs disappear and the layer beneath is revealed.
      • A mismatch costs you 5 seconds.
      • Clear every layer of every tile to win.
