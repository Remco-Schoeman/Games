Best pours

    The two-part pour

    • Hold to pour until the stout reaches the harp etched on the glass — three quarters full.
    • Release and let it surge: the bubbles separate into a creamy head and a dark body. Wait for it.
    • Hold again to top off, stopping the moment the head domes just over the rim.
    • Score is the average of the two stops, with a bonus for waiting through the full settle and a penalty for overflowing.
